## USER INFO

Key controls: C to toggle on/off placement mode. In placement mode, P to place primitives, L to place trees, R to place rocks, and F to place flags. V to toggle on/off particle effect when placing. Camera angle can always be moved/scrolled when not dragging *on* the terrain surface.
